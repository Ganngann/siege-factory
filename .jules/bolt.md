## 2024-07-23 - Lazy Initialization of Occupied Crops Query
**Learning:** In spatial queries within `src/agriculture/cultivator.rs`, `find_plantable_tile_spiral` dynamically created an `occupied_crops` `HashSet` by iterating over `crops` on every call, even though many candidate tiles are not free and could be short-circuited. This created an O(N²) bottleneck when called per-frame for multiple idle cultivators.
**Action:** Lift the collection logic into an `Option<Vec<(i32, i32)>>` outside the inner loop and evaluate it lazily using `Option::get_or_insert_with` only when other fast checks (like `spatial.is_free`) pass. By using a pre-sorted vector and `binary_search` instead of a `HashSet`, we avoid the overhead of constructing the `HashSet` and its large allocation costs for every cultivator. Apply this lazy instantiation and sorted `Vec` pattern whenever spatial logic is frequently executed and relies on slow collections.
